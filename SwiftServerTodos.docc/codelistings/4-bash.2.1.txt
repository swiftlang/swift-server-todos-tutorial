Select "Loki" from the data source drop-down.

In the query box, enter:

  {service_name="SwiftServerTodos"}

Press "Run query". Every log line the app emitted shows up here. Lines
written while a request was being handled carry trace_id and span_id
fields — expand a line to see them. Clicking the trace_id jumps straight
to the corresponding trace in Tempo.

Startup and migration logs are emitted before any request span exists, so
they don't have a trace_id.
