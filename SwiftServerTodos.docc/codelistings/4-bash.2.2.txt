Select "Tempo" from the data source drop-down.

Open the "Search" tab and set Service Name to SwiftServerTodos, then
press "Run query". The list on the right shows recent HTTP requests;
click any row to open the full trace.

Inside the trace, the Vapor request span wraps its Fluent child spans.
Use the "Logs for this span" link to jump back to Loki and read the
log lines written while that request was being handled.
